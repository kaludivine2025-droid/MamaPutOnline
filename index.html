Our Story

Bringing Mama's Kitchen to Your Doorstep

Founded in 2018 by Mama Nkechi, a passionate cook from Anambra State, MamaPutOnline started as a small kitchen serving neighbors and friends. Today, we are Lagos's most trusted online Nigerian restaurant, delivering authentic homemade flavors to over 50,000 satisfied customers across the city.

Every soup is stirred with patience, every rice dish is seasoned with generations of tradition, and every meal is packed with the warmth of a Nigerian mother's love. We do not just cook food -- we preserve culture, one pot at a time.

Frequently Asked Questions

How long does delivery take?
We deliver within 45 minutes in Lagos, 60 minutes in Abuja, and 90 minutes in Port Harcourt. During peak hours (12-2pm, 6-8pm), delivery may take up to 15 minutes longer.
Is there a minimum order amount?
Yes, our minimum order is ₦2,000. However, delivery is FREE for all orders above ₦5,000. For orders below ₦5,000, a delivery fee of ₦500 applies.
Can I customize my order?
Absolutely! You can add special instructions in the notes section before checkout. Want extra pepper? Less oil? No problem! Our chefs are happy to accommodate your preferences.
Do you cater for events and parties?
Yes! We cater for weddings, birthdays, corporate events, and family gatherings. Contact us at least 48 hours in advance for orders above 20 plates. We also provide serving staff and equipment for large events.
What payment methods do you accept?
We accept cash on delivery, bank transfer, USSD, and all major debit/credit cards. You can also pay via Paystack on our website or mobile app for secure, instant transactions.
